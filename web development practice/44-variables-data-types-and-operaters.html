Operaters in JavaScript.

There are two types of operaters:

  1. Unary Operater:
    • It has single operand(x = -x)
  2. Binary operater:
    • It has two operands (x = x + 6)

What is operand?

The entities on which operaters can operate is called operand.

For Example:

In 3 + 4 (+) is the operater and (3 and 4) are operands.
