Bodmas And Operater Precedence

  1. Complex arithmatic expressions can pose a problem when there aremultiple operaters in single expression.
  2. var a = 5 + 2 * 3 - 2 / 2;//result 10.
  3. The evaluation of above expression is depends on BODMAS rule.

BODMAS RULE

  1. B
    • Brackets First
  2. O
    • Orders(i.e powers and square roots, etc)
  3. DM
    • Division and Multiplication (Left to Right)
  4. AS
    • Addition and Subtraction(Left to Right).

Eliminating ambiguity -- BODMAS

  • var a = 5 + 2 * (3 - 2) / 2;//result 6

    1. 3 - 2 with brackets will be evaluated first, result 1.
    2. 2 * result of (3 - 2) si 2 * 1, result 2.
    3. Result of 2 * (3 - 2) devided by 2 so 2 / 2, result 1.
    4. 5 + result of 2 * (3 - 2) / 2, so 5 + 1, result 6
